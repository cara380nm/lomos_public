The southern city of Brevnia is widely renowned as a cultural hub. From the nobility's richly ornamented palaces that dominate the skyline down to the fine goods its artisans export, the city is considered a symbol of class.

The creative spark manifests in other ways too. Brevnia has a bohemian underclass of artists and bards who, while they might not seen as appropriate in court, can start movements and influence the destiny of kingdoms.
